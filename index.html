üé¨ How to Add Your YouTube Shorts:

  1. Go to your YouTube channel: https://youtube.com/@yaminitharite-r4h
  2. Find a Short you want to add and click on it
  3. Copy the video ID from the URL:
    From: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ABC123XYZ
    Video ID: ABC123XYZ
  4. Replace the placeholder with this code:
    <div class="short-container">
      <iframe class="youtube-short"
        width="315"
        height="560"
        src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID"
        frameborder="0"
        allowfullscreen>
      </iframe>
    </div>
  5. Replace "YOUR_VIDEO_ID" with your actual video ID

Example: If your Short URL is https://www.youtube.com/shorts/dQw4w9WgXcQ, then replace YOUR_VIDEO_ID with dQw4w9WgXcQ
